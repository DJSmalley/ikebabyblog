Today was a big day for Isaac. Not only did he turn eight months old, but he also crawled for the first time. We set him down on one side of the room, turned on his favorite music-and-flashing-lights toy on the other side, and he crossed the great divide under his own power. Doug cheered and hugged him and I cried. Our little boy is growing up so fast.

February 11, 2004

It's Croup!

Isaac is sick--very sick. He came down with croup (http://kidshealth.org/parent/infections/lung/croup.html) last night, which meant he slept very little and Doug and I slept even less. Three days of labor was hard, but hearing Isaac in pain and struggling for breath was 1000 times harder.

We went to the doctor's today and she gave him a steroid shot. We hope it works and he's able to rest tonight and play more tomorrow.
