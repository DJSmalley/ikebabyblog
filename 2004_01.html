January 15, 2004

Practicing 'Round the Clock

In his ever-vigilant quest to figure out how to crawl, Isaac has been practicing constantly--even in his sleep. He pushes himself up onto his hands and knees every time he rolls over on to his stomach, so at 2 a.m. (and 4 a.m., and 6 a.m. ...) he's up on all fours, rocking back and forth, getting more and more excited every second. This wouldn't be a problem, except he can't go back to sleep until one of us gets out of bed and helps him roll back over. What will it be like when he's learning how to walk?!
