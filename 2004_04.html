The Joy of Discovery

During the past few weeks, Isaac has really become much more engaged and fascinated with his surroundings. He is constantly pointing at and wanting to touch anything and everything -- trees, phones, toothbrushes, grass, kitties, bananas, fans, books ... basically whatever catches his eye.

There is nothing like the feeling of watching your son smile and giggle with delight when presented with his first dandelion. We are so blessed to be witnesses to this ongoing miracle. Thank you for choosing us little bean.
